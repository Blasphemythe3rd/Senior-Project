Test Instructions

Task Overview

You will see sequences of numbers or a mix of numbers and letters
Ordering Rules:
  • Order the numbers in ascending order (smallest to largest)
  • When shown a mix of numbers and letters, the sequence should begin with numbers ascending then letters alphabetically

Examples:

Numbers only: Given: 9 3 7 2 Correct order: 2 3 7 9
Mix of numbers and letters: Given: F 2 R S Correct order: 2 5 F R

Test Format

Ordering Rules:
  • The test has 12 sequences with 2 practice trials first.
  • Click symbols in the correct order to respond
  • Audio & Visual feedback will be provided to help guide you.
